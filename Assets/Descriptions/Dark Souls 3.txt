As fires fade and the world falls into ruin, developer FROMSOFTWARE and director Hidetaka Miyazaki continue their critically-acclaimed and genre-defining series with Dark Souls III. Fans and newcomers alike will get lost in the games hallmark rewarding gameplay and immersive graphics. Now only embers remain... Dynamic lighting and particle effects immerse players into a dark fantasy world of ruin. Expertly crafted world encourages players to explore vast and breathtaking landscapes. Diversifies combat action allowing players to craft their own unique play style. Evolution of trademark online multiplayer functionality that seamlessly integrates online interactions into single player story.
