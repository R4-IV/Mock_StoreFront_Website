Righteous retribution can be yours. Take control of the iconic “Ghost of Sparta” Kratos as he rains carnage and destruction upon the deities who have betrayed him.

Armed with Kratos’ deadly double-chained blades, rip through mythology’s most vicious creatures and annihilate anything which gets in your way of destroying Olympus and the mighty Zeus himself.

Set in the fantastic realm of Greek mythology, God of War III Remastered brings the critically acclaimed PS3 adventure roaring into life and on to PlayStation 4. Rise from the darkest depths of Hades and scale the very heights of Mount Olympus in stunning 1080p visuals running at 60 frames per second.
